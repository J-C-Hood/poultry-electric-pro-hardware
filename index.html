Case Knives

W.R. Case & Sons Cutlery has established a legacy as an American knife maker of high quality, extremely collectible handmade knives. Case pocket knives, fixed blade knives, and other premium cutlery have been proudly passed down from grandfather to grandson for decades. We’re proud to carry their full product line in addition to offering limited edition Case XX knives, exclusively available through Poultry Electric & Pro Hardware.
